YinzerAI

A tiny AI that translates plain English into Pittsburghese, right on your device. No server, no sign-up, no jaggin' around.

Wait, how does this work? And why does it sometimes say something completely unhinged?

This is a real, custom-trained AI language model built by Dev4PGH. When you first visit, it downloads the model files to your device and runs the AI directly on your phone or computer. Nothing you type is ever sent to a server. After the first load, it should start up much more quickly from your device's cache.

It is genuinely good at Pittsburghese most of the time. But it is also tiny by AI standards, so occasionally it slips up, goes sideways, or decides to translate its own instructions instead of your text. Consider it part of the charm. Yinz have been warned, n'at.
